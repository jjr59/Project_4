Performance

The 2024 Mustang GT is equipped with a powerful 5.0-liter V8 engine, delivering 480 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque, with an optional Performance Package that pushes output to 500 horsepower. The GT can accelerate from 0-60 mph in 4.2 seconds and reaches a top speed of around 155 mph. It offers both a 6-speed manual and a 10-speed automatic transmission, giving drivers the choice between a classic or modern shifting experience.

Interior Features

The GT model’s interior blends sporty elements with comfort-focused features, creating a cabin that is both functional and luxurious. With premium materials and advanced technology, the Mustang GT’s interior provides a seamless experience for drivers and passengers alike. Key features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The 2024 Mustang GT exudes classic Mustang style with modern aerodynamic enhancements, including a bold grille, sculpted hood, and quad exhaust tips. The GT model comes with standard 18-inch alloy wheels, with options for 19-inch or 20-inch wheels in the Performance Package. The GT also includes a rear spoiler, and optional appearance packages add racing stripes and unique badging.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

Ford’s Co-Pilot360 suite of safety and driver-assistance features comes standard on the Mustang GT, offering enhanced security on the road. Safety systems include:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
