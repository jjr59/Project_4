Performance

The 2024 Mustang GT Fastback is powered by a 5.0-liter V8 engine that delivers 450 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, giving it a 0-60 mph time of about 4.2 seconds. With a choice between a 6-speed manual and a 10-speed automatic transmission, the GT Fastback offers versatility for different driving styles. The car's performance is further enhanced by selectable drive modes, including Normal, Sport, Track, and Drag Strip, allowing drivers to customize handling and responsiveness.

Interior Features

The Mustang GT Fastback’s interior combines classic muscle car aesthetics with modern technology, creating a sporty and comfortable environment. Premium materials and thoughtful design details give the cabin a refined feel. Key features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The Mustang GT Fastback boasts a sleek, aerodynamic design that enhances both style and performance. Its exterior features iconic Mustang elements like the bold grille, sculpted hood, and signature tri-bar taillights. The Fastback includes 18-inch alloy wheels, with options to upgrade to 19-inch or 20-inch wheels. The dual exhaust with quad tips adds a sporty touch, while an available rear spoiler enhances downforce and stability.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

Ford’s Co-Pilot360 technology suite comes standard on the Mustang GT Fastback, blending performance with comprehensive safety features. Standard systems include:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
