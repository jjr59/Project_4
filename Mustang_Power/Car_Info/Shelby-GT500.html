Performance

The Shelby GT500 is engineered for exhilarating performance with its supercharged 5.2-liter V8 engine, delivering an astounding 760 horsepower and 625 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse achieves a 0-60 mph time of just 3.3 seconds and can reach top speeds exceeding 180 mph. It comes exclusively with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, ensuring seamless, rapid gear changes for maximum on-road or track performance.

Interior Features

The GT500’s interior combines advanced tech and racing-inspired luxury, crafted to create a driver-focused cockpit. It offers RECARO® racing seats with Miko® suede inserts, designed for comfort and control during high-performance drives. Other interior features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The 2024 Shelby GT500 sports an aggressive, track-inspired look with aerodynamic enhancements. A functional hood vent, larger front splitter, and rear spoiler optimize downforce, while unique 20-inch carbon-fiber wheels provide durability and lightweight performance. Shelby badges, racing stripes, and distinct Shelby badging emphasize its iconic status.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

Balancing high-speed thrills with comprehensive safety, the Shelby GT500 incorporates Ford’s Co-Pilot360 technology, including advanced safety systems:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
