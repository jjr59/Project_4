Performance

The 2024 Mustang Boss 302 is powered by a specially tuned 5.0-liter V8 engine, delivering 450 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. Known for its responsive handling, the Boss 302 is optimized for track performance, with a 0-60 mph time of around 4.2 seconds and a top speed of 155 mph. It is available with a 6-speed manual transmission, built to give drivers a direct and exhilarating driving experience.

Interior Features

The Boss 302 interior blends retro-inspired style with modern Mustang technology, emphasizing both driver comfort and control. The cabin features distinct Boss 302 badging and Alcantara accents, with race-inspired details. Key features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The Boss 302 exterior reflects its racing heritage, featuring aggressive styling and functional aerodynamics, including a front splitter, rear spoiler, and exclusive Boss 302 decals. It comes with unique 19-inch alloy wheels and a dual exhaust system with active valve performance tuning. Optional racing stripes are available, paying homage to the original Boss 302 legacy.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

The Boss 302 is equipped with essential safety features, blending performance with Ford’s Co-Pilot360 technology for added security. Standard safety systems include:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
