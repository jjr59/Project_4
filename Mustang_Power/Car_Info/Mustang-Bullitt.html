Performance

The 2024 Mustang Bullitt features a specially tuned 5.0-liter V8 engine, delivering 480 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque, designed to honor the legacy of Steve McQueen’s classic. It achieves a 0-60 mph time of around 4.1 seconds and can reach a top speed of 163 mph. Paired exclusively with a 6-speed manual transmission, the Bullitt model emphasizes a pure driving experience with features like a cue ball shift knob and rev-matching technology for smooth downshifts.

Interior Features

The Bullitt’s interior offers retro-inspired details combined with modern Mustang technology, making it a tribute to the 1968 original. The cabin is outfitted with premium materials and unique accents, creating a distinctive and immersive environment. Key features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The Bullitt’s exterior is designed to evoke the look of the original movie car, with a Dark Highland Green paint option exclusive to the model. It features minimal badging for a clean, classic appearance, with blacked-out accents and unique 19-inch alloy wheels. The Bullitt also includes chrome trim on the grille and windows, a cue ball shifter, and an active quad-tip exhaust system with a unique sound profile.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

The Bullitt model is equipped with Ford’s Co-Pilot360 suite of safety and driver-assistance technologies, providing peace of mind alongside its high-performance appeal. Standard safety features include:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
