Performance

The Shelby GT350 packs a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter flat-plane crank V8 engine, producing a powerful 526 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque. This beast goes from 0-60 mph in just 4.0 seconds and can reach a top speed of approximately 173 mph. Available with a 6-speed Tremec® manual transmission, the GT350 emphasizes precision driving, catering to enthusiasts who prioritize an engaging, high-performance experience.

Interior Features

The GT350's interior design combines track-ready function with refined styling, offering a cockpit centered around the driver. With optional RECARO® racing seats and custom interior stitching, the GT350 provides both comfort and performance. Key features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The GT350's exterior is built for aerodynamics and high-speed stability, with an aggressive front splitter, vented hood, and rear spoiler to optimize downforce. It also includes unique 19-inch alloy wheels and prominent Shelby badging. Optional racing stripes and distinctive Shelby styling accents enhance its iconic look, setting it apart from other Mustang models.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

Equipped with a range of driver assistance and safety features, the GT350 is designed to balance its performance prowess with comprehensive safety. It comes standard with Ford’s Co-Pilot360, which includes:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
