Performance

The 2024 Mustang EcoBoost is powered by a turbocharged 2.3-liter inline-4 engine, producing an impressive 310 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, with an optional High-Performance Package boosting power to 330 horsepower. The EcoBoost model achieves a 0-60 mph time of 5.0 seconds and a top speed of approximately 145 mph. It offers both a 6-speed manual and a 10-speed automatic transmission, providing versatility for everyday driving or spirited performance.

Interior Features

Inside, the Mustang EcoBoost is crafted to offer a modern and comfortable environment, with a design that reflects the Mustang’s sporty heritage. The cabin includes refined materials and advanced technology features for both driver and passenger convenience. Key features include:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The 2024 Mustang EcoBoost combines iconic Mustang styling with aerodynamic enhancements, including a bold front grille, sleek lines, and dual exhaust tips. The EcoBoost model also comes with 18-inch alloy wheels, with optional upgrades to 19-inch wheels and a rear spoiler. Optional appearance packages add unique badging and decals, enhancing the Mustang’s classic appeal.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

The EcoBoost model is equipped with Ford’s Co-Pilot360 suite of safety features, balancing performance with everyday protection. Standard safety systems include:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
