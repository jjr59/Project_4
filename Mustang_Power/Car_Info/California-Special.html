Performance

Equipped with a robust 5.0-liter V8 engine, the California Special trim delivers an impressive 480 horsepower and 410 pounds of torque. It boasts a swift 0-60mph acceleration time of just 4.3 seconds and can reach speeds exceeding 160mph. Choose between a 6-speed manual and a 10-speed automatic transmission to suit your driving preferences.

Interior Features

For 2024, Ford has reimagined the Mustang interior with a fighter jet-inspired sleek design. The GT trim now features upgraded leather inserts, enhancing the cabin sophistication. The California Special stands out with its Navy Pier and black leather upholstery, accented with stylish blue stitching. The interior is further adorned with GT/CS floor mats and a unique California Special badge. From the Premium trim, it inherits features like:

  • A 12.4-inch Digital Instrument Cluster
  • A 13.2-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Ambient Lighting
  • Particulate Air Filtration
  • Aluminum Pedals
  • A Leather-wrapped Shift Knob
  • Power-Adjustable, Heated, and Cooled Front Seats

Exterior Features

The 2024 Ford Mustang GT boasts an aggressive, aerodynamic design that turns heads on the road. Its sleek lines and iconic Mustang styling cues exude power and presence. The California Special adds to this with distinctive decals across its lower body panels and exclusive 19-inch alloy wheels.

Safety & Driver Assistance Systems

The Mustang GT California Special is equipped with a comprehensive suite of driver-assistance technologies, balancing high-speed performance with advanced safety. Ford Co-Pilot360, standard across all Mustang models, includes:

  • Auto High-Beam Headlamps
  • AdvanceTrac® with Electronic Stability Control (ESC)
  • SOS Post-Crash Alert System
  • Multiple Airbag Systems
  • LATCH Anchors for Children
  • Personal Safety System™ for Driver and Front Passenger
  • Safety Canopy® Side-curtain Airbags
  • 3-point Safety Belt Restraint System
  • Adaptive Cruise Control
  • Lane Departure Warning and Lane Keeping Assist
